Our Team

We're a diverse team with backgrounds in technology,
marketing and finance.

Joshua Levine

CEO/Co-founder

Josh has a long history in financial technology, from institutions as a Managing Director in Technology at Morgan Stanley to retail traders at E*TRADE as their CTO, to asset managers as the CEO of ESP Technologies (acquired by ITG) and now for private companies at Sharewave. He is on the board of several companies, including the online grocer FreshDirect and non-profits, including DonorsChoose.org helping classrooms in need.

Robert M. (r0ml) Lefkowitz

CTO/Co-founder

Robert is a technologist with a history of making information easier to access and analyze. He built financial trading systems at Morgan Stanley and Merrill Lynch, analytics at AT&T Wireless and Asurion, and visualizations at 1010data and Root Markets. He's an ACM Distinguished Engineer and frequent speaker at Open Source conferences.

Alan McGee

CMO/Co-founder

Alan has a history of assembling data to tell better stories about product and customer growth. He's overseen digital marketing and analytics at multiple organizations, including at DonorsChoose.org when they were named one of Fast Company's "50 Most Innovative Companies in the World." He also volunteered in Kenya as a Kiva Fellow, spent two years as an iMentor in the NYC public school system, and is a former mechanical engineer.

Alison Iven

Creative Director

Alison is a design generalist who has experience in web and application development, brand communications, and illustration. She studied graphic design at the University of Cincinnati. In her free time, she can usually be found digging holes in her community garden or riding her moped.

Robert Walport

Product Manager

Robert was born in Blighty, growing up in London and eventually reading Physics at Oxford. He jumped across the pond for a pair of master's degrees, Journalism and Computer Science, at Columbia University before joining the Sharewave team in January 2013. He previously took a brief sojourn into the arts working at the Brooklyn Academy of Music (BAM), but he had to talk to far too many people so decided software engineering was a safer pursuit.

Anthony F. Carillo

CFO

Anthony has 17 years of experience as a CPA specializing in the financial services industry. He has worked as an Audit Manager for KPMG Peat Marwick; Financial Reporting and Risk Manager for ITOCHU International; VP for Merrill Lynch; and Controller for a small biotech company, International BioImmune Systems. Anthony is our resident "ironman" having finished 22 ironman races including 6 times racing the Hawaii Ironman World Championships.
