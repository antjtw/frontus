Software Engineer

Overview

We are building a technology platform that’s a beautifully simple way for private companies to organize, visualize, and share information with their investors.

We’re looking for an engineer to work directly with our CTO to build the product from the ground up. Our technical challenges are more about flexibility and customization and less about scalability. We need to do many small things, not one massive thing. If you’re a great problem solver and synthesist, and love diverse challenges, we would love to hear from you.

We’re a team of 9 people with financial, technology, and startup experience (we’ve worked at places like E*TRADE, Deloitte, Accenture, Morgan Stanley). We’ve already attracted a very experienced Board, consisting of C-level executives from market-leading companies within finance and marketing. We've validated demand for our product through discussions with venture capital firms, private equity firms and Fortune 500 companies.

We’re working in the city’s newest co-working space in midtown Manhattan, which hosts a number of startups and small businesses, along with unlimited coffee, tea and beer.

Responsibilities/Skills

  • Hands-on front-end and/or back-end web development
  • Working Knowledge of one of the following: Java, Python
  • Proficiency with web and mobile technologies ( OAuth, REST, Gesture APIs, NoSQL)
  • Excellent problem solving skills

Experience

  • CS degree or related experience
  • Demonstrable software development experience
