The example above returns both the monetary value and resulting ownership in the company (we've removed currency symbols so it's more friendly to everyone). The graph is a visualization of the effective discount on conversion, which gets interesting when the convertible note has a valuation cap.
For simplicity, we've rounded the Conversion Value to the nearest whole number and limited the precision of Ownership to the thousandths place. We've also witheld additional terms that can influence conversion, such as interest rate, which are available within Sharewave when you're running this against your own data. That means the math is also applicable to Safes, which were introduced by YCombinator.
