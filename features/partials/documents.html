Prepare Your
Document

There's too much back-and-forth in signature flows, so we've made it simple. Just highlight data input areas, identify who will complete the entry, and define what you're asking for. You'll pre-sign the document, which we'll hide from your recipient until the document is executed.

Recipient Signature

We'll identify the pages where their information is needed, and highlight the fields you've created in the correct format. Once they've signed, you'll get a notification to review the document.

Approve & Execute

It's as simple as that, and your signature is back in place and waiting for execution. If rejected, the document will head back to the recipients with any comments you have. If approved, we'll lock down the document with an appended audit trail and notify you and your recipient.

Safely request signatures

Your recipients receive an email inviting them to securely access their unique version of the document. Sending to 5 people will create 5 independent versions, each with their own history.

Legally binding audit trail

Appended to each executed document is a full timeline of interactions containing dates, times, email addresses, IP addresses and other document actions taken along the way.

Share multiple documents

Easily share groups of documents that require signatures, so your recipients are only notified once and can sign everything in one session.
