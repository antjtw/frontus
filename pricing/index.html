Common questions

You charge for documents. How do you define a document?

A document is any item that is uploaded to Sharewave, as well as any document signed and executed via Sharewave. However, inviting people to view a document is limitless.

What if I go over my plan limits?

You will not be charged extra during the month that you exceed your plan limit, nor will we restrict the product. In the following month, you will be automatically upgraded to a higher plan.

What is a direct message?

A one-way email message to a person or group of people, ideal for sending company information while keeping your personal inbox clean.

What is an admin?

An admin is any user with edit rights. You can invite unlimited shareholders and non-shareholders to view & sign documents, and view cap table and grant information, with every plan.

Can I cancel my subscription?

If you no longer wish you use Sharewave, you can cancel at any time. At that point, your subscription will last until the end of the current monthly billing cycle.

If I leave the service, can I export my data?

Yes. Sharewave provides a service to make your data more valuable, but you are the owner of your information. Should you decide to leave, we'll make your data available to download.
