Document Management

  • Are e-signatures on Sharewave legally binding?
  • Yes, though currently in the United States only. We append an audit trail with IP Address and activity to each document, which is court admissible.
  • What document types can I upload?
  • Right now we only take PDF documents, though we'll soon accept a wider range.
  • How do I know if someone has viewed a document?
  • When viewing the document, select "Status" from the navigation to view an audit trial by recipient.

Understand Your Ownership

  • What percent of the company do my shareholders own?
  • Just enter names, amounts, and terms. Leave the math to us.
  • How much of my option pool have I given to my employees?
  • You enter the total shares authorized, and we tell you what's left unissued.
  • Can I show my employees the vesting progress on their stock options?
  • Yes, simply enter their vesting schedule and share a personal view of the cap table with them.
  • If I share my cap table, what can the recipient see?
  • Shareholders will only see their personal holdings by default, though you can grant them an unlimited view of the cap table via their profile.
