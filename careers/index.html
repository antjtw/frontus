The role

Your mission at Sharewave will be to act as a catalyst who improves the effectiveness of our application developers. This certainly includes:

making things run faster
making it harder for things to fail
making it easier to figure out what went wrong
making it easier to test things
making it easier to deploy changes

We're looking for

Working Knowledge of one of the following: PostgreSQL, Python, Haskell, Java
Experience with Linux (we use Ubuntu)
Experience with Amazon Web Services (EC2)
Experience building, updating, deploying, monitoring, and/or testing web applications
Track record of practical problem solving
Effective communicator
